Scrambled Eggs
eggs are my favorite
Ingredients: Method:
  • 2 eggs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Butter or oil for cooking
  • Crack the eggs into a bowl and whisk them until well combined.
  • Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  • Pour the whisked eggs into the skillet.
  • Stir gently with a spatula until the eggs are softly scrambled.
  • Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  • Serve immediately.
